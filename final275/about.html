VoyagerSC

USC - Uniting Student Creatives. With the never-ending talent on our sunny campus and the driven minds that set lofty project goals to make a difference, the team at VoyagerSC wanted to create a platform that connected the two. Whether you are a graphic designer, video editor, script writer, or even a musician, this is the place for you to collaborate with passionate project managers who share a common goal. It's simple - sign up as a freelancer, create your portfolio, and collaborate with any student you wish on your idea!
